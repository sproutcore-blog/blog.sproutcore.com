We, on the SproutCore Team, would like to introduce the community to our newest Core Team member: Michael Cohen!  Michael has been a very enthusiastic member of the SproutCore Community and has release some amazing code for SproutCore.  He is the main author and designer of the [Lebowski Framework](https://github.com/FrozenCanuck/Lebowski).  Lebowski was developed to use-case test large SproutCore applications namely [Eloqua10](http://www.eloqua.com/eloqua10/). He has worked extensively with Core Team members: Mike Ball (@onkis) and Evin Grano (@etgryphon), on the statechart implementation for SproutCore (formerly called Ki).

Michael has also been a incredible community member and helping people understand how to learn SproutCore with his blog: [Keeping Sanity One Smelting Accident at a Time](http://frozencanuck.wordpress.com/).  He is currently employed at [Eloqua](http://www.eloqua.com/) as a Senior Developer along with other core team members: Evin Grano and Mike Ball.
